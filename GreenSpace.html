Green Space, A Frame of Mind in our Industrial Outdoors - 2020

Published in ‘Emerge Magazine’ Issue #2 (2021) and exhibited at Pallas Project/Studios, the work features a still image containing a QR code link to an immersive 360 degree video, available to be viewed regularly or through a VR headset. The immersive video displays an idealised nature space, with a digital recreation of the Aindriú running around it. The piece considers what we have come to view as nature, calling into question the word ‘natural in itself.


The virtual space was created digitally using ‘Unreal Engine 4’, the recreation of the artist’s body was hand modelled using both ‘Mudbox’ and ‘Blender.
