Artist Bio

Aindriú Ó’Deasún is a young artist living and working in both Antwerp and Dublin. Having just graduated his BA in Fine Art Media + Visual Cultures at NCAD, he has worked under a range of circumstances, from interning for new-media artist Elaine Hoey, to living in the deep rural artists residency ‘Brown Mountain Diamond’. Aindriú’s practice is extremely versatile, constantly in search of new materials, spaces and ideologies to expand his work as well as the works of those around him.
