Catching Something Moving at the 'Creative Factory' Turnhout - 2023

Shown at the Creative Factory in Turnhout, this reworking of Aindriú's graduating show Catching Something Moving saw the work on a much larger scale, situated in a 200sqm gallery space.


While the video piece and general concept remained the same, the larger space allowed for a freestanding iteration of the inflatable polythene structures, a much larger screen at 2x1.8m and a spatial audio piece.


The work was redeveloped and constructed at Atlier SKTLU in Antwerp centre, the show at Creative Factory titled , was curated by Mark Lissens, this work was featured alongside paintings from Ella Goris and JW Van de Haele.
